Create Memories That Last a Lifetime

Lekki Conservation Centre, Lagos:

Experience nature at its finest at the Lekki Conservation Centre, home to diverse wildlife, lush vegetation, and the iconic canopy walkway.

Details :

  • Location: Lekki - Epe Expressway, Lagos, Nigeria
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Adults: NGN 1,000 Children (3-10): NGN 200

Olumo Rock, Abeokuta:

Climb the ancient Olumo Rock for panoramic views of Abeokuta and explore the historic caves and shrines nestled within its rugged terrain.

Details :

  • Location: Olumo Rock, Itoku, Abeokuta, Nigeria
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Adults: NGN 1,000 Children: NGN 500

Yankari National Park, Bauchi:

Discover the natural beauty and wildlife diversity of Yankari National Park, home to elephants, lions, and picturesque warm springs.

Details :

  • Location: Yankari, Bauchi State, Nigeria
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Adults: NGN 2,000 Children: NGN 1,000

Nike Art Gallery, Lagos:

Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Nigerian art at the Nike Art Gallery, showcasing traditional and contemporary artworks from local artists.

Details :

  • Location: Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Free entry; donations appreciated
