Viking Ship Museum, Oslo:

Explore well-preserved Viking ships dating back to the 9th century. Witness Viking history and culture firsthand through artifacts and exhibitions.

Details :

  • Location: Huk Aveny 35, 0287 Oslo, Norway
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM Saturday and Sunday: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Adults: NOK 120 Children (under 18): Free

Bergen Fish Market, Bergen:

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of Norway's most famous fish market. Sample fresh seafood and local delicacies while enjoying scenic views of Bergen's waterfront.

Details :

  • Location: Strandkaien 3, 5013 Bergen, Norway
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Saturday: 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM Sunday: Closed
  • Admission Fee: Free entry

Geirangerfjord, Møre og Romsdal:

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Norway's UNESCO-protected Geirangerfjord, known for its stunning landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and pristine natural surroundings.

Details :

  • Location: Geirangerfjord, Møre og Romsdal, Norway
  • Opening Hours: Open year-round for sightseeing, but activities and tours may vary by season.
  • Admission Fee: Free entry to public viewpoints; fees may apply for guided tours and activities.

Norwegian Glacier Museum, Fjærland:

Learn about glaciers and their impact on the environment through interactive exhibits and multimedia displays. Discover the history of glaciers in Norway and their significance.

Details :

  • Location: Olavsbakken 2, 6848 Fjærland, Norway
  • Opening Hours: May to September: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (daily) October to April: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM (closed on Mondays)
  • Admission Fee: Adults: NOK 150 Children (6-15): NOK 50
