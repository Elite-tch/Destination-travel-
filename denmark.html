Tivoli Gardens, Copenhagen:

Experience the magic of one of the world's oldest amusement parks, featuring thrilling rides, enchanting gardens, and lively entertainment for all ages.

Details :

  • Location: Vesterbrogade 3, 1630 København V, Denmark
  • Opening Hours: Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM Friday and Saturday: 11:00 AM - 12:00 AM Sunday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Admission Fee: Adults: DKK 140-140 Children (3-7): DKK 50-100

Nyhavn, Copenhagen:

Stroll along the iconic waterfront of Nyhavn, lined with colorful historic buildings, bustling cafes, and lively atmosphere. Enjoy boat tours, dining, and street performances.

Details :

  • Location: Nyhavn, Copenhagen, Denmark
  • Opening Hours: Open year-round for sightseeing, restaurants and cafes have varying opening hours.
  • Admission Fee: Free entry to public areas; fees may apply for boat tours and attractions.

Kronborg Castle, Helsingør:

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site of Kronborg Castle, famously known as the setting for Shakespeare's Hamlet. Discover the history, architecture, and stunning views from the fortress.

Details :

  • Location: Kronborg 2C, 3000 Helsingør, Denmark
  • Opening Hours: May to September: 10:30 AM - 5:00 PM (daily) October to April: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM (closed on Mondays)
  • Admission Fee: Adults: DKK 130 Children (6-17): Free

Legoland Billund Resort, Billund:

Immerse yourself in the colorful world of LEGO at Legoland Billund Resort, featuring themed rides, interactive attractions, and impressive LEGO sculptures.

Details :

  • Location: Nordmarksvej, 7190 Billund, Denmark
  • Opening Hours: Open seasonally; check website for current hours
  • Admission Fee: Prices vary depending on season and package; check website for details
