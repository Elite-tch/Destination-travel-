Colosseum, Rome:

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater and one of Rome's most iconic landmarks, known for its historical significance and impressive architecture..

Details :

  • Location: Piazza del Colosseo, 1
  • Opening Hours: 8:30 AM to 7:15 PM daily
  • Admission Fee: €16 for adults and free for children under 18.

Venice Canals, Venice:

The Venice Canals are a network of picturesque waterways, famous for their romantic gondola rides and charming bridges.

Details :

  • Location: Historic city of Venice
  • Opening Hours : Open 24 hours
  • Admission Fee: no fees

Leaning Tower of Pisa, Pisa:

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a unique bell tower known for its distinctive tilt, attracting visitors from around the world.

Details :

  • Location: Piazza del Duomo, Pisa.
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM (April - September), 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (October - March)
  • Admission Fee: €18 for adults, €15 for children aged 8-18, free for children under 8

Florence Cathedral (Duomo), Florence:

The Florence Cathedral, also known as the Duomo, is a magnificent Renaissance church featuring iconic red dome and beautiful artworks.

Details :

  • Location: Piazza del Duomo, Florence
  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 4:30 PM (Monday - Friday), 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Saturday), 1:30 PM - 4:45 PM (Sunday)
  • Admission Fee: €18 for adults, free for children under 12
